Your suite at Excellence El Carmen

Adults-only, all-inclusive, beachfront — priced for two adults, seven nights, room only.

Excellence El Carmen opened in late 2016 on Uvero Alto beach, a quieter stretch of Punta Cana's coast north of the busier Bávaro zone. It's an all-suite, adults-only property — 492 suites spread along a wide ribbon of golden sand, with 23 pools and a dozen restaurants. Your Excellence Club suite unlocks the resort's best layer of service, and the Excellence Club Beachfront Honeymoon Two-Story Rooftop Terrace Suite with Plunge Pool gives you a private plunge pool that no one else can use.

What Excellence Club includes

  • Private Excellence Club Lounge, open 7:00 am–11:00 pm, with front desk service and smart buffets
  • A private Excellence Club beach and pool area
  • Access to Magna, the members-only restaurant
  • Bvlgari bath amenities, an upgraded minibar and room-service menu, and a premium in-room liquor and wine selection
  • Daily fresh fruit and petit fours, plus a complimentary beach bag to use during your stay
  • One day pass per stay to Excellence Punta Cana or Finest Punta Cana, transportation included (arranged at the front desk)
An honest note on getting there. Uvero Alto is roughly a 40–45 minute drive north of Punta Cana International (PUJ) — longer than the resorts clustered nearer the airport. The trade is a calmer, less crowded beach with far fewer vendors working the sand. Your round-trip airport transfer is included at this length of stay (3-night minimum; you're at seven). This quote is room only — flights aren't included, so book the air that works for you, or tell me and I'll price options.

The suite

Excellence Club · Adults-only · Two floors + rooftop terrace

Excellence Club Beachfront Honeymoon Two-Story Rooftop Terrace Suite with Plunge Pool

Your own plunge pool, two floors up, with the sunset to yourselves.

An exclusive corner of quiet luxury, set right on the beach within the Excellence Club. The suite climbs two floors: separate living quarters and a spa-like marble bathroom below, then a sprawling private rooftop terrace above with its own plunge pool, lounge furniture, an outdoor shower, and an open line to the sunset. It's conceived specifically with romance in mind — outfitted with the full range of Excellence Club services.

One honest note on the rooftop pool: it's a plunge pool — waist-deep, sized for soaking and cooling off rather than swimming laps — and it's a climb up to the terrace. What you get in return is a pool no one else can use and a private vantage for sunset. Excellence lists the suite at roughly 1,600 square feet, counting the full two-floor envelope plus the rooftop terrace, so a good share of that is generous outdoor living.
