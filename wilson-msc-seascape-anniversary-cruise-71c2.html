Your Trip at a Glance

Seven nights, three countries, one anniversary worth marking.

The MSC Seascape departs Galveston Sunday evening and makes its way south through two sea days before arriving in Costa Maya on Wednesday morning — giving you time to settle in before the ports start. The itinerary then moves through Roatan's reef-lined Bay Island on Thursday, Cozumel's clear shallows on Friday, and a final sea day Saturday before arriving back in Galveston Sunday at 7:00 a.m.

MSC Seascape is one of the line's newest ships, launched in late 2022 and designed with more outdoor deck space per passenger than most in its class. The onboard experience — dining options, entertainment programming, the Yacht Club private complex — skews toward guests who want a polished product without the scale of the mega-ships.
