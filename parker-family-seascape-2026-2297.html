Costa Maya, Mexico

A quieter stretch of the southern Yucatán coast, with a beach complex built right at the pier and the Chacchoben ruins a short excursion inland. Less crowded than Cozumel — a full 10 hours in port to fill at your own pace.

Isla de Roatan, Honduras

One of the Caribbean's best-value dive and snorkel destinations. The Mesoamerican Barrier Reef hugs the island's north shore — clarity and marine life rival far more famous sites. Between the reef, zip-line canopy tours, and the kids-focused West Bay beach, this tends to be the most activity-dense port on the route.

Cozumel, Mexico

The Palancar Reef is the anchor — world-class diving and snorkeling in some of the clearest water in the Caribbean. San Miguel town runs a solid craft market and several good restaurants. The most established port on this itinerary, which means everything runs on schedule.

Understanding the Experience Tiers

Same ship, same balcony category. Very different what comes with it. Here's the honest breakdown before you choose.

Bella

Entry Experience

  • Balcony cabin guaranteed — deck and location assigned by MSC
  • Fixed-time main dining (set seating, set time)
  • Full access to all ship amenities and entertainment
  • Lowest price on every cabin tier
Important: MSC selects your cabin's specific location and deck — you're guaranteed a balcony, but not where on the ship you land. Not recommended for groups with multiple rooms who want to be near each other.

Best for: travelers who want the balcony experience at the lowest rate and are comfortable letting MSC decide their spot.

Fantastica

Standard Experience

  • Choose your deck tier — BR1 (Decks 9–10) through BR4 (Deck 15)
  • Higher deck = slightly larger balcony and higher vantage point
  • Flexible dining: choose your preferred time slot
  • Priority boarding
  • Butler call service
  • Full ship amenity access

Best for: most families. Deck-selection control is the key advantage over Bella — meaningful when three rooms need to be in the same area of the ship.

Aurea

Premium Experience

  • Specific cabin assigned (not just cabin type guaranteed)
  • Any-time dining — no set schedule, dine when you like
  • Aurea Spa thermal suite access included
  • Priority embarkation and boarding
  • Premium deck positioning (Deck 9+)
  • Dedicated concierge services

Best for: travelers who want spa access built in, complete dining flexibility, and the security of a specific assigned cabin.

Advisor note: verify exact Aurea spa inclusions and any OBC promotions on this sailing before confirming with client.

MSC Voyagers Club & Status Match

You may already have loyalty status that transfers — and I can help you use it.

MSC Voyagers Club is MSC Cruises' loyalty program. Members earn points on every sailing and unlock perks that scale with tier — onboard credits, spa discounts, priority boarding, welcome cocktails, and more. Tiers run from Classic through Classic Plus, Silver, Gold, Gold Plus, Diamond, Diamond Plus, and Black.


Status Match Program: If anyone in your group holds elite status with another cruise line or hotel loyalty program, MSC will often match you to a comparable Voyagers Club tier — so you step aboard with benefits already in place rather than starting from scratch.

Cruise lines that commonly qualify:
Royal Caribbean Crown & Anchor  ·  Carnival VIFP  ·  Celebrity Captain's Club  ·  Norwegian Latitudes  ·  Holland America Mariner Society

Hotel programs that commonly qualify:
Marriott Bonvoy  ·  Hilton Honors  ·  World of Hyatt  ·  IHG One Rewards  ·  Wyndham Rewards
