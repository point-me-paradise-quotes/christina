Before You Sail

Good to Know

A few housekeeping notes so embarkation day feels effortless.

Travel Documents

All guests must carry a valid passport plus any visas or immunizations required for the itinerary. Each guest is responsible for identifying and presenting the required documents at boarding and upon re-entry to the United States.

Web Check-In

Online check-in opens 30 days before sailing and closes 24 hours prior. Completing it early speeds up your priority Yacht Club check-in at the pier, which opens at 1:00 PM on embarkation day.

Travel Insurance

MSC recommends every guest carry adequate travel insurance. If you have not yet secured coverage, let me know using the form below — I can walk you through the options before you sail.

eTickets

Tickets are issued at least 21 days before departure, once all guest data is on file and payment is complete — your payment is done, so you are well ahead of the game.

Excursions & Extras

Shore excursions, spa appointments, and other extras can be added anytime through Manage Booking on the MSC website — or simply ask me and I will handle it for you.

Disembarkation

The ship arrives back in Galveston at 7:00 AM on November 1. If you are planning any onward arrangements, allow 2–3 hours for disembarkation due to operational needs.
