Trip Overview

5 Nights, Western Caribbean

Round-trip from Galveston · July 6 – July 11, 2026

Mariner of the Seas runs a tight 5-night loop out of Galveston — Western Caribbean ports, back by Saturday morning. At 138,000 gross tons and just over 3,800 guests at full capacity, it's large enough that 9 people can scatter across the ship on a sea day and still find a quiet corner. The FlowRider wave simulator and rock climbing wall draw the under-14 crowd from mid-morning through early afternoon. The adults get the pool decks — or the adults get the FlowRider, no judgment. Onboard dining runs from the main dining room's three-course service to the Windjammer's buffet, which peaks around noon and clears out by 1:30. At the ports, the itinerary is yours — no group flag to follow, no bus schedule to keep.

Cabin Configuration

Your Staterooms Aboard

Five cabins · All remaining availability is double occupancy

With quad and triple cabins sold out on this sailing, we configured five double-occupancy staterooms to keep the family together across two deck levels. The connecting pair on Deck 8 puts the 13-year-old with one parent and the 8-year-old with the other — a private interior door between the two rooms means the kids can move freely without going into the hall. On Deck 2, three ocean-facing staterooms handle the grandparents, the youngest child, and the remaining two adults. Every cabin in the group carries a $50 onboard credit from Point Me to Paradise.
