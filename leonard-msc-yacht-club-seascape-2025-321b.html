The Trip

MSC Seascape, Yacht Club

The MSC Seascape sails a 7-night Western Caribbean loop out of Galveston, Texas, calling on Costa Maya and Cozumel in Mexico and Isla de Roatán in Honduras — with two sea days bookending the ports. The Yacht Club is MSC's ship-within-a-ship concept — a private deck, a dedicated pool, butler service, and a top-deck restaurant that's yours alone. Below are nine sail date options spanning November 2026 through January 2027. Each lists the same cabin category (Yacht Club Deluxe Grand Suite) with its specific pricing and any calendar notes relevant to your schedule.
