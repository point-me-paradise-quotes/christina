Option 1 · Poipu, south shore · 1 villa · 2 adults, 2 kids

Koloa Landing Resort at Poipu, Autograph Collection

A one-bedroom villa, a full kitchen, and the pool that keeps winning Best in America.

This is the rare family room that isn't a room at all — it's an 80-square-metre one-bedroom villa with a separate bedroom (a door that closes, a king for the two of you), a queen sofa bed for the kids in the living area, a full kitchen, and an in-room washer/dryer. For a week with a 6- and 8-year-old, that's the difference between everyone on top of each other and everyone with their own corner.

The pool is the headline, and it's earned: Koloa Landing's 350,000-gallon main pool has been voted Best Pool in America six years running (2020–2025), with an infinity edge, swim-through grottos, and two waterslides (one for all ages, one for kids 48 inches and up). There's a separate 6,100-square-foot family lagoon pool with a basketball hoop, a quieter adults' pool, and five hot tubs. Poipu Beach — calm, swimmable, with resident sea turtles — is a few minutes away on Kauai's sunny south shore.

Option 2 · Princeville, north shore · private home · 2 adults, 2 kids

Princeville Bluff House — a private home on the north shore

Three bedrooms, a real kitchen, and 3,500 square feet to spread out in.

This is the option for a family that wants a house, not a hotel room. This home is a single-level, ~3,500-square-foot home in one of Princeville's resort communities — three separate bedrooms, three full baths, vaulted ceilings, and walls of windows framing ocean, mountain, and golf-course views. With room for eight here, the four of you have more space and privacy than any resort here can offer: the kids get their own rooms, you get yours, and there's a bonus room with a sofa bed on top.

The covered lanai is built for open-air dinners over the fairways and the Pacific, the kitchen is fully equipped for real cooking, and you're steps from the Makai Golf Course and minutes from Hanalei Bay, Anini Beach, and Queen's Bath. It's the north shore at its most residential and relaxed.

Primary bedroom with a king, ensuite, and soaking tub; a second king bedroom with ensuite; a queen guest bedroom; and a bonus room with a sofa bed. For a family of four, that's a bedroom each for the kids and room to spare.

Honest trade: a house gives you the most space, privacy, and kitchen of anything here — three real bedrooms versus one room with a sofa bed at the resorts. Option 3 · Poipu, south shore · oceanfront condo · 2 adults, 2 kids

Poipu Point Oceanfront Residence — oceanfront condo

A condo's space and kitchen, with a waterslide pool and athletic club thrown in.

This one threads the needle between a private rental and a resort. It's a two-bedroom, two-bath oceanfront condo (~1,400 sq ft) perched on the Poipu coastline, with a front-row lanai over the water, AC throughout, a full kitchen, and a washer/dryer. Two king bedrooms plus a queen sofa bed sleep your family of four with room to spare, and traditional shoji doors give real separation between the bedrooms and living space.

What sets it apart from a standalone rental: guests get access to the Poipu Beach Athletic Club (for up to five), which gives you the resort amenities a condo usually lacks — a resort-style pool with a waterslide, a hot tub, tennis, pickleball, basketball, a full gym, and the Cabana Bar & Grill. There's also an ocean-view pool, tennis/pickleball, and a community BBQ right on site. Sunny Poipu's beaches are minutes away.

Option 4 · Poipu, south shore · oceanfront villa · 2 adults, 2 kids

Poipu Coast Corner Villa — oceanfront end unit

The most space and the highest-end finish of the rentals — a true oceanfront corner unit.

If the condo idea appeals but you want more room and a quieter, more polished setting than the Poipu Point condo, this is it. This is a ~1,800-square-foot, two-level oceanfront corner villa in a boutique-luxury setting in the heart of Poipu — the largest of the rental options. The great-room level opens to an oceanfront lanai with AC and ocean views throughout; upstairs, two king suites each with their own full bathroom (the primary is oceanfront with a Jacuzzi tub).

The complex keeps it low-key but well-equipped: an ocean-view pool, tennis and pickleball courts, multiple BBQ areas, even an herb garden. You're minutes from Poipu's beaches, shops, and dining on Kauai's sunny south shore. Full kitchen, washer/dryer, AC and ceiling fans in every room.
