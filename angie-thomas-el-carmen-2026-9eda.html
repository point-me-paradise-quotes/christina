One resort, three ways to stay

All three suites are Excellence Club, adults-only, all-inclusive — priced for two adults, seven nights, room only.

Excellence El Carmen opened in late 2016 on Uvero Alto beach, a quieter stretch of Punta Cana's coast north of the busier Bávaro zone. It's an all-suite, adults-only property — 492 suites spread along a wide ribbon of golden sand, with 23 pools and a dozen restaurants. Every suite here starts at 800 square feet and comes standard with an oversized in-suite jacuzzi for two, a furnished terrace, and 24-hour room service.

The three suites below sit at the top of that ladder. All are Excellence Club, all beachfront, all honeymoon-category. What changes between them is the room itself — how much space you have, and how your private outdoor area works. Read each one, then choose your suite and cancellation terms below; the running total updates as you go.

A quick note on the square footage you'll see below: Excellence measures the full suite envelope — indoor living space plus the private terrace, and, on the two-story suites, the rooftop deck and pool area. So the larger numbers reflect generous outdoor living, not just interior rooms.

What Excellence Club includes — on all three suites

Booking an Excellence Club suite unlocks the resort's best layer of service. Every option below comes with:

  • Private Excellence Club Lounge, open 7:00 am–11:00 pm, with front desk service and smart buffets
  • A private Excellence Club beach and pool area
  • Access to Magna, the members-only restaurant
  • Bvlgari bath amenities, an upgraded minibar and room-service menu, and a premium in-room liquor and wine selection
  • Daily fresh fruit and petit fours, plus a complimentary beach bag to use during your stay
  • One day pass per stay to Excellence Punta Cana or Finest Punta Cana, transportation included (arranged at the front desk)
An honest note on getting there. Uvero Alto is roughly a 40–45 minute drive north of Punta Cana International (PUJ) — longer than the resorts clustered nearer the airport. The trade is a calmer, less crowded beach with far fewer vendors working the sand. Your round-trip airport transfer is included at this length of stay (3-night minimum; you're at seven). This quote is room only — flights aren't included, so book the air that works for you, or tell me and I'll price options.

Option 1 · Excellence Club · Adults-only · Beachfront, single level

Beachfront Honeymoon Swim-Up Suite

Step off your terrace straight into the water.

This is the suite for people who want the water at their feet the moment they wake up. At around 1,000 square feet — interior plus an expansive private terrace — it sits right on the beach, with a separate living-room area and a terrace that opens directly onto a semi-private pool connected to your building's main pool. You're not walking to the pool — you're stepping off your own terrace into it.

Inside, it's a full marble bathroom with a rain shower built for two, double vanities, and an oversized jacuzzi. Sparkling wine and fresh flowers are waiting when you arrive. Of the three, this is the most relaxed footprint and the easiest in-and-out of the water — a single ground-floor level, no stairs, the beach a few steps past the pool.

Best for: couples who live in the water and want it effortless — pool access without leaving the suite, beach a few steps beyond, everything on one level.

Option 2 · Excellence Club · Adults-only · Two floors + rooftop terrace

Beachfront Honeymoon Two-Story Rooftop Terrace Suite

Your own plunge pool, two floors up, with the sunset to yourselves.

This is the one I'd point to first. For roughly sixteen dollars a night more than the swim-up, you go from about 1,000 to roughly 1,600 square feet — and from a shared building pool to one that's entirely your own. That footprint is split across two floors plus the rooftop: separate living quarters and the marble bath below, then a private rooftop terrace above with its own plunge pool, lounge furniture, an outdoor shower, and an open line to the sunset.

The trade against Option 1 is honest and worth understanding: you gain privacy, space, and a pool no one else can use, but you give up stepping straight from your terrace into the water. The rooftop pool is a climb up, and it's a plunge pool — waist-deep, sized for soaking and cooling off rather than swimming laps. For most couples on a honeymoon-style week, that trade lands firmly in this suite's favor. Same beachfront position, same Excellence Club access as the others.

Best for: couples who want privacy and room to spread out over raw water access — a pool that's yours alone and a rooftop built for sunsets, at a small step up in price.

Option 3 · Excellence Club · Adults-only · Largest suite · Two floors + rooftop

Imperial Suite Two-Story Rooftop Terrace with Plunge Pool

The most space on the property, and the highest tier of service.

The Imperial is the largest of the three by a wide margin — Excellence lists it at roughly 3,400 square feet counting the full two-floor envelope and the rooftop terrace. The lower level holds a full living room with its own bar, the bedroom, and the marble bath; the rooftop carries the private plunge pool, a lounge bed, and a sofa area. It's the same rooftop-pool concept as Option 2, but with noticeably more room to move through, indoors and out.

What truly separates it is the service layer. The Imperial adds VIP touches on top of standard Excellence Club: nightly turndown, daily fresh fruit and petit fours in the suite, and one complimentary hour of in-suite barman service during your stay (arranged through the front desk 24 hours ahead) — think a private bartender setting up cocktails for one special evening, rather than an all-week butler. If you want the suite itself to be a destination — somewhere you'd happily spend a full afternoon — this is that room.

Best for: couples who want the suite to be part of the experience — maximum space, a private bar, and elevated touches, with the same rooftop plunge pool as Option 2.
